Hotels in Hong Kong (REGIONS)

Whether you are visiting the 'Gateway to China' for business or leisure, we have Hong Kong accommodation located throughout the Kowloon Peninsula, Hong Kong Island, and the New Territories. A melting pot of cultures, Hong Kong has so much to offer, from local and international culinary delights to great shopping and business opportunities. Stay in our Hong Kong Island accommodation to experience some of the regions most spectacular views. Visit one of Hong Kong's most popular tourist attractions, Victoria Peak, which is 552m above sea level and has views of the city and the bay. Or visit Victoria Harbour, which is one of the world's busiest harbours with around 220,000 ships visiting each year. Our Hung Hom accommodation is a perfect base if you are conducting business, or studying at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, or for visiting Whampoa Garden. Our Kowloon hotels allow you to appreciate all the city has to offer: from the spectacular Chi Lin Nunnery Buddhist temple, to the many inexpensive Cantonese and Chiu Chow eateries; from the Hong Kong Space Museum to the 500 exhibits at the Hong Kong Science Museum. Our Mong Kok hotels, on the Kowloon Peninsula, are perfect for experiencing all the shopping and entertainment opportunities of the Mong Kok district, which means 'flourishing or busy corner?in Chinese. Or stay in our Tsim Sha Tsui accommodation to experience this bustling neon shopping district which offers a plethora of international cuisine and local cuisine. Or stay in our Yau Ma Tei accommodation, in the south of the Kowloon Peninsula, to visit Yung Shue Tau square to experience a bustling night market, which takes place every night, selling everything from clothes to VCDs and toys. Stay in our hotels in the New Territories and see the world's sixth largest suspension bridge, the Tsing Ma Bridge, the world's tallest outdoor seated bronze Buddha statue, the Tian Tan Buddha at Ngong Ping, and Hong Kong Disneyland, completed in 2005. If you are in Hong Kong on business we have hotels in Wan Chai, Hong Kong's busiest commercial area, which is just a ferry ride away from Kowloon City. If Hong Kong is only a quick stopover destination for you, stay in our Hong Kong Airport hotels, and visit the Citygate Shopping Centre, just a short taxi ride from the airport, or take a 13 minute train ride to Maritime Square Shopping Centre and make the most of your short time in Hong Kong.
